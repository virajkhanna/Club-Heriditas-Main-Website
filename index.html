About Club Hereditas


Club Hereditas aims to raise awareness about the importance of heritage in the world and why heritage conservation is of crucial importance. The Heritage Club has planned workshops, talk shows, presentations, competitions, etc. over the upcoming year to achieve this very goal. In addition, the club has also planned a series of Heritage Walks, where all club members are taken to places of rich cultural and historical significance in areas around the Delhi NCR.

Club Hereditas


Heritage refers to the customs, traditions, beliefs, and artefacts that are passed down from generation to generation within a particular culture or society.There are many different types of heritage, including natural heritage (such as national parks and wildlife), cultural heritage (such as museums and historic buildings), and intangible heritage (such as traditional knowledge and practices). India's cultural identity is second to none. Having borne witness to innumerous ancient civilizations and being the birthplace of some of the brightest minds to have walked on Earth, our country's heritage is crucial to our cultural identity and woven into the fabric of our society. Keeping this in mind, the school would like to announce the creation of its Heritage Club -- Club Hereditas..
